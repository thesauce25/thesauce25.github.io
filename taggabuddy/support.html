Tagga Buddy is a personal NFC tag-tapping app where one creature responds when you visit places you care about.

Getting started

  1. Open the app and name your creature.
  2. Stick an NFC tag somewhere meaningful: gym, kite spot, end of a trail, your front door.
  3. Tap "Write new tag" and hold your phone to a blank NFC tag to register it. Give it a name and a goal.
  4. The next time you visit, hold your phone to the tag. Your creature reacts and you earn points.

Common questions

What kind of NFC tags work?

Any NDEF-writable NFC tag works. NTAG213, NTAG215, and NTAG216 are all good. You can find them on Amazon for a few dollars.

I tapped a tag and nothing happened.

Make sure the tag was registered first via "Write new tag." Tapping an unregistered or blank tag will not score points. Also confirm NFC is on in iOS Control Center.

Will the creature die if I don't tap?

No. The creature falls asleep after about four days without a tap, but it cannot be lost. The next tap wakes it up.

Can I sync across devices?

No. Tagga Buddy is on-device only. Your tags and creature live on the iPhone the app is installed on.
