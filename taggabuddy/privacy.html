Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-03

Tagga Buddy is a personal NFC tag-tapping app. It does not collect any data about you.

What we collect

Nothing. The app stores your creature's name, the tags you register, and your tap history entirely on your device. None of that information is transmitted to any server.

What we share

Nothing. The app has no analytics, no advertising, no third-party SDKs, and no network calls beyond what the operating system provides for universal links.

Permissions

  • NFC reader: used only to read NFC tags you have registered with the app. Tag reads happen on your device and stay on your device.

Data retention and deletion

All data stays on your device. Deleting the app removes everything.

Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. It does not collect any data from anyone.
